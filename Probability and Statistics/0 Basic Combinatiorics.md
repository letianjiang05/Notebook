# 1.3 Permutations
- 6 men 4 women: $ 10! $
- 6 men 4 women ranked: $ 10! / (6! \times 4!) $
- PEPPER Permutation: $ \frac{6!}{3! \times 2!} $

# 1.4 Combinations
Choose 3 from 10: $ \binom{10}{3} = \frac{10!}{3! \times (10-3)!} = \frac{10!}{3! \times 7!} $
